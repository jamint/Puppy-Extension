Warrior Canine Connection

Puppy Enrichment Center

Boyds, Maryland

Warrior Canine Connection gives Golden and Labrador Retriever puppies the early nurturing and training they need to develop into life-changing service dog partners for combat Veterans with disabilities.

The puppies learn to love, trust and bond with humans beginning at about age 4 months.
