Blossom's Litter

Bergin University of Canine Studies

Rohnert Park, CA

This is Blossom's second litter with Bergin University. Blossom is a 3 year old golden retriever. The sire of this litter is Odin, a handsome 2 year old black lab. All pups will be raised and trained at Bergin University.

Part of Bergin University's mission is to train service dogs for people with physical disabilities and veterans with combat-related disabilities.
